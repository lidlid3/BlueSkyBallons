What's it really like to ride in a hot air balloon?


You don't feel motion

Unlike a plane or helicopter, in a hot air balloon there really is no sensation of movement, because it is so slow and gentle. The landing is as soft as your grans chocolate chip cookies.

You get a bird's eye view

It is quite an adventure to see the world from 500 to 2,000 feet up (our normal flight levels). The flight won't bring your acrophobia to new heights.

You can hear everyhing

Flying respectively close to the ground, you can hear dogs barking, cats meowing, and cars honking at you. You can also hear the jealous of everyone because they aren't you.
