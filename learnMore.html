Who we are

Blue Sky Balloons is a family company dedicated to offering the best hot air balloon rides in Middle Tennessee. Since 1985, our balloons have embelished the Nashville skies all year round. Peter, Jenny, and their three kids love to fly and decided it was time to open up their own company so others can enjoy the experience. Don't miss out, come soar with us!

Who will fly you.

Our staff is committed to delivering an impecable performance for our customers. Our three pilots have completed around 500 balloon rides all together.
They are the best at what they do, and we are proud to have them in the Blue Sky Balloon family!

John

John is our resident comediant! Customers describe their rides with John as the most fun they have had being above the ground.

Martha

Martha has been a Blue Sky Balloon pilot for most of our time flying over the Nashville skies. Her love and knowledge of the city definetely shows through in her rides.

Tim

Tim is a hot air balloon expert and enthusiast. He is our best man when it comes to fixing any issue with our balloons. Customers who want to know the science behind our rides always ask for him.
